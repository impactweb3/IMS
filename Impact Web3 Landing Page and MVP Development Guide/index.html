Transforming Impact Measurement in Blockchain

Impact Web3 provides a standardized framework to measure, track, and report the social, environmental, economic, and governance impacts of blockchain projects.

Social Impact

Measure effects on well-being, quality of life, and social dynamics within communities.

Environmental Impact

Track effects on the natural environment, sustainability, and resource efficiency.

Economic Impact

Evaluate influence on economic growth, job creation, and financial inclusion.

Governance Impact

Assess contributions to improving governance structures and processes.

Key Features

Our Impact Measurement System is designed with scalability, interoperability, and sustainability at its core.

User-Friendly Interface

Straightforward forms and questions aligned with Project Catalyst's simple application and reporting processes.

Comprehensive Data Collection

Collect data on social, economic, environmental, and governance impacts through standardized metrics.

Flexible & Scalable

A system that scales with ecosystem growth while maintaining a set of core indicators.

Open Standards & APIs

Facilitate integration with other platforms, enabling use by NGOs and external partners.

Feedback Mechanisms

Continuous improvement through user feedback and refinement of the measurement framework.

Multi-Chain Compatibility

Built on Cardano and Midnight, with compatibility for EVM and other blockchain technologies.

Impact Metrics

Our system tracks key indicators across multiple dimensions to provide a comprehensive view of project impact.

Ecosystem Growth & Decentralization

  • Governance participation growth over time
  • Number of unique proposers in each funding round
  • Percentage of wallets that voted in each round
  • Number of self-sustaining projects
  • Number of independent partnerships formed

Social & Environmental Impact

  • Number of funded projects addressing social issues
  • Reduction in carbon footprint of operations
  • Percentage of SPOs using renewable energy
  • Number of participants in capacity-building programs
  • Resource efficiency improvements

Economic Empowerment

  • Financial inclusion increase in underrepresented regions
  • Number of unique wallet addresses in underserved regions
  • Direct and indirect job creation
  • Revenue generated by projects
  • Number of businesses benefiting economically

Technological Adoption

  • Percentage of transactions from Catalyst-funded projects
  • Number of dApp interactions and unique users
  • Partnerships with commercial entities or government agencies
  • Adoption rate across different sectors
  • Growth in businesses integrating blockchain solutions
